Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Our sports injury rehabilitation program is designed to help athletes of all levels recover quickly and safely from injuries. We use a combination of targeted exercises, manual therapy, and cutting-edge techniques to address your specific needs and get you back in the game stronger than ever.

  • Comprehensive injury assessment and diagnosis
  • Customized rehabilitation plans tailored to your sport and goals
  • Sport-specific training and conditioning programs
  • Injury prevention strategies and education
  • Performance enhancement techniques
  • Collaboration with coaches and trainers for optimal recovery

Manual Therapy

Our manual therapy services provide targeted, hands-on treatment to address a wide range of musculoskeletal issues. Our skilled physiotherapists use various techniques to manipulate and mobilize your joints and soft tissues, promoting healing and improving function.

  • Joint mobilization and manipulation techniques
  • Soft tissue massage and myofascial release
  • Trigger point therapy and dry needling
  • Stretching and strengthening exercises
  • Postural correction and ergonomic advice
  • Pain management strategies

Postural Correction

Poor posture can lead to a range of health issues, including back pain, neck strain, and reduced mobility. Our postural correction program is designed to address these issues and improve your overall posture and wellbeing.

  • Comprehensive posture assessment and analysis
  • Customized exercise programs to strengthen weak muscles
  • Stretching routines to improve flexibility
  • Ergonomic advice for work and home environments
  • Manual therapy to address muscle imbalances
  • Education on maintaining good posture in daily activities

Careway Pathway

The Careway Pathway is a fully funded ACC program providing surgical or non-surgical pathways for specific injuries such as shoulder tears, dislocations, and knee injuries (ACL and dislocations).

  • Comprehensive assessment and diagnosis of eligible injuries
  • Tailored treatment plans for optimal recovery
  • Regular progress monitoring and adjustments
  • Coordination with specialists and surgeons when necessary
  • Pre-surgery preparation and education (if applicable)
  • Post-surgery rehabilitation and return-to-activity guidance

Acupuncture/Dry Needling

Our trained physiotherapists use acupuncture and dry needling to target specific points in the body, helping to relieve pain, improve circulation, and promote natural healing processes.

  • Pain relief for acute and chronic conditions
  • Improved muscle function and flexibility
  • Enhanced blood flow and tissue healing
  • Stress reduction and relaxation benefits
  • Complementary treatment for various musculoskeletal issues
  • Personalized treatment plans combining acupuncture with other therapies
