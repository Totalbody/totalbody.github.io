Our Services

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Specialised treatment to address your specific needs and goals.

Our sports injury rehabilitation program helps athletes of all levels recover and return to peak performance. We use targeted exercises, manual therapy, and cutting-edge techniques tailored to your sport and body.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques to improve mobility and reduce pain.

Our manual therapy services include joint mobilisation, soft tissue massage, and myofascial release to enhance physical function and alleviate pain.

Postural Correction

Customised programs to improve your posture and prevent injuries.

We assess your posture and develop tailored exercise programs to correct imbalances, reduce pain, and improve overall body alignment.

Careway Pathway

ACC-funded program for specific injuries.

Careway is a fully funded ACC program providing surgical or non-surgical pathways for specific injuries such as shoulder tears, dislocations, and knee injuries (ACL and dislocations). Our expert team guides you through your recovery journey.

Acupuncture/Dry Needling

Effective techniques to alleviate pain and promote healing.

Our trained physiotherapists use acupuncture and dry needling to target specific points in the body, helping to relieve pain, improve circulation, and promote natural healing processes.

About Total Body Physio

Over the years, we've grown our practice not just in size, but in the depth and quality of care we offer. Our growth has always been driven by our desire to help more people, not simply to increase profits. We believe that by expanding our reach, we can positively impact more lives and contribute to the overall health and wellbeing of our community.

At Total Body Physio, we pride ourselves on our hands-on approach. We combine expert manual therapy with personalised exercise programs, always focusing on the individual needs of each patient. Our style is complemented by what we call our 'best practices' - a blend of evidence-based techniques and our collective experience that ensures the highest standard of care.

We're not just physiotherapists; we're partners in your health journey. Our team's personality shines through in every interaction, creating a warm, welcoming environment where healing and improvement flourish. We're committed to continual learning and improvement, always striving to bring the latest and most effective physiotherapy techniques to our practice.

Choose Total Body Physio for care that goes beyond treatment - we're here to educate, support, and empower you on your path to optimal physical health.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect during my first visit?
During your first visit, we'll conduct a thorough assessment of your condition, discuss your medical history, and develop a personalised treatment plan.
Do I need a referral to see a physiotherapist?
No, you don't need a referral to see a physiotherapist. However, if you're claiming through ACC, you may need a referral from your doctor.
What should I wear to my appointment?
Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy access to the area being treated. For lower body issues, shorts are recommended.
How long does a typical physiotherapy session last?
A typical session lasts about 30-60 minutes, depending on the treatment required.
Is physiotherapy covered by ACC?
Yes, ACC covers physiotherapy for accident-related injuries. You may need to pay a surcharge for each visit.
