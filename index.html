About Total Body Physio

Founded in 2000, Total Body Physio was born from a passion for helping people achieve their best physical health. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful goal: to provide exceptional physiotherapy care that truly makes a difference in people's lives.

Over the years, we've grown our practice not just in size, but in the depth and quality of care we offer. Our growth has always been driven by our desire to help more people, not simply to increase profits. We believe that by expanding our reach, we can positively impact more lives and contribute to the overall health and wellbeing of our community.

At Total Body Physio, we pride ourselves on our hands-on approach. We combine expert manual therapy with personalised exercise programs, always focusing on the individual needs of each patient. Our style is complemented by what we call our 'best practices' - a blend of evidence-based techniques and our collective experience that ensures the highest standard of care.

We're not just physiotherapists; we're partners in your health journey. Our team's personality shines through in every interaction, creating a warm, welcoming environment where healing and improvement flourish. We're committed to continual learning and improvement, always striving to bring the latest and most effective physiotherapy techniques to our practice.

Choose Total Body Physio for care that goes beyond treatment - we're here to educate, support, and empower you on your path to optimal physical health.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect during my first visit?
During your first visit, our expert physiotherapist will conduct a comprehensive assessment. This includes discussing your medical history, current symptoms, and goals. We'll perform a physical examination to evaluate your condition. Based on this assessment, we'll develop a personalised treatment plan tailored to your specific needs and explain the proposed course of action.
Do I need a referral to see a physiotherapist?
In New Zealand, you generally don't need a referral to see a physiotherapist for private treatment. You can book an appointment directly with us. However, if you're seeking treatment for an ACC-covered injury, you may need a referral from your GP or specialist. If you're unsure, give us a call, and we'll be happy to guide you through the process.
What should I wear to my appointment?
We recommend wearing comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy access to the area being treated. For lower body issues, shorts or loose trousers are ideal. For upper body concerns, a tank top or loose shirt works well. If you're coming directly from work, don't worry - we can provide gowns or shorts if needed.
How long does a typical physiotherapy session last?
A typical physiotherapy session at Total Body Physio lasts between 30 to 60 minutes. Initial consultations may be longer, usually around 45-60 minutes, to allow for a thorough assessment. Follow-up sessions are generally 30-45 minutes. The exact duration can vary based on your specific condition and treatment needs.
Is physiotherapy covered by ACC?
Yes, ACC covers physiotherapy for accident-related injuries in New Zealand. If your injury is ACC-approved, a significant portion of your treatment cost will be covered. You may need to pay a small surcharge for each visit, which varies depending on your specific case. We can assist you with the ACC claim process and provide more details about any associated costs during your initial consultation.
