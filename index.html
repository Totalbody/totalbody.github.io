About Total Body Physio

Founded in 2000, Total Body Physio has been at the forefront of physiotherapy care in Auckland for over two decades. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful goal: to provide exceptional physiotherapy care that truly makes a difference in people's lives.

Over the years, we've grown our practice not just in size, but in the depth and quality of care we offer. Our growth has always been driven by our desire to help more people, not simply to increase profits. We believe that by expanding our reach, we can positively impact more lives and contribute to the overall health and wellbeing of our community.
